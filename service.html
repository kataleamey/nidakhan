What You’ll Get Inside the Program

This 90-day program is designed to help you build emotional strength, gain respect from your husband and in-laws, set healthy boundaries, and live a happy and peaceful life.

Program Phases:

Phase Description
Phase 1 Foundation Call to understand your relationship dynamics and begin emotional healing
Phase 2 Manage your emotions, improve communication, and gain respect from your husband and in-laws
Phase 3 Learn to set boundaries, overcome jealousy, and improve mental fitness
Phase 4 Live a healthy married life and build a strong foundation for a lasting relationship

Program Structure

  • Phase 1: Foundation Call - Understand relationship dynamics, challenges, and set the groundwork for emotional healing.
  • Phase 2: Develop Self Love - Cultivate emotional intelligence and resolve conflicts effectively.
  • Phase 3: Communication - Learn assertive communication and let go of jealousy to build strong self-worth.
  • Phase 4: Thrive Your Marriage Or Fill The Communication Gap - Break negative cycles, adapt to a growth mindset, and foster healthy communication with your spouse/in-laws.

Additional Bonuses

  • 30 Days Extra Accountability - For long-lasting, sustainable results.
  • Ho'oponopono Healing Meditation - To reduce stress, embrace compassion, and foster self-love.
  • Tools for Maintaining a Long-Lasting Healthy Relationship - Learn effective tools to nurture your relationship and personal growth.

What You’ll Achieve

  • Develop emotional strength and confidence to handle any relationship challenge.
  • Build and maintain healthy relationships with your husband and in-laws.
  • Learn how to attract positivity and let go of negativity in your life.
  • Gain respect and appreciation from your family, leading to a happier and more fulfilling marriage.
